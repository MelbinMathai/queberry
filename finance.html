Banking & Insurance

Queberry is designed to serve the complex financial customer experience in a pleasant and friendly way by minimizing actual and perceived waiting time using mobile-first technology. Online Appointment Scheduling Software For Banks is an easy & innovative solution to handle different stages of a customer journey, from pre-arrival to the end of the journey, using the latest smart technologies.

Queberry Internet and location-based mobile applications inform the customer to find the nearest branch and take a virtual ticket booking and appointment scheduling before arriving on site.

Engage your customers by building
Personalised Relationships

Customer profiles

By creating detailed customer profiles in the appointment management system for banking, you can personalise each customer’s experience to make them feel valued and special.

Accessibility

Since appointment scheduling software for banks is accessible to everyone, customers feel connected and prioritised. This helps the bank to build a personalised relationship with the customer.

Tailored services

Banks can use the scheduling software to gain insights into customer behaviour and preferences, allowing them to provide tailored services and build a personalised relationship with the customer.

Easy Appointments
Solution for your clients

Bank appointment scheduling is now easier than ever. With this, clients can schedule and reschedule appointments, and receive appointment reminders.

An appointment management system for banking helps banks save time and money and improve customer service. It reduces the need for manual scheduling, streamlines appointment scheduling processes, and eliminates costly no-shows.

Our software also allows banking staff to quickly access customer information and history, making it easier to provide the best customer service possible. With the right appointment scheduling software, banks can ensure clients get the service hassle-free.
