QB-KO-1101

The QB-KO-1101 is a multi-purpose interactive self-service outdoor kiosk with a bright 32″ touch panel with a proper fat enclosure. QB-KO-1101 is highly durable and high-performance, built for 24×7 operation. QB-KO-1101 is designed for multi-channel payment models and is equipped with a cash acceptor, card reader, scanner and NFC reader.

QB-KO-1101 – Digital Self Service Outdoor Kiosks Solution offers Wide-range and flexible integration capabilities and supports a multi-channel payment model.

Feature of
QB-KO-1101

Multi-Channel Payment Model

QB-KO-1101 interactive outdoor kiosk is designed for a multi-channel payment model with a cash acceptor, card reader, scanner, and NFC reader.

High-Quality Touchscreen Displays

QB-KO-1101 kiosk system offers a 32” High Brightness TFT LCD touch-screen interface with a bright sunlight-readiness display for the outdoor environment.

Improve Operations

QB-KO-1101 offers an uninterruptible power supply, access control & anti-theft alarm for your customers and supports wide-range and flexible integration capabilities.

80 mm Thermal Printer

QB-KO-1101 outdoor kiosks are integrated with a standard 80mm thermal printer to deliver environmentally friendly printing, and Paper tickets support graphics, logos, and text.

Quicker Service

By providing speedy checkout and efficient service, you can satisfy your valuable customers and prevent you from losing yourself to the competition.

Alerts and Notifications
