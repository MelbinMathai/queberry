Counter Display

Counter Display System is an add-on component beneficial for customer flow management with improved signage showing tickets, counter number display along with digital media.

Features of
Counter Display

10.4′′ Screen Size

We offer you a complete selection of Counter Display Systems, including 10.4′′ Screen Sized industry-leading and innovative solutions.

Viewing Angle: Full View

The counter display provides a modern designed, full-view display system to display token numbers and data to enhance functionality.

WIFI Enabled

Token display systems offer Wi-Fi-enabled solutions for customer management, showing tickets, counter numbers, and digital media.

Quad Core Processor

The counter number display system has a powerful quad-core processor to provide a high-performance value equation and a unique customer solution.

Supports Ceiling, Desk and Wall Mount

The counter display system is designed for easy installation on any ceiling, desk, or wall mount to display the token number and counter number.

Self Service Kiosk

The next generation high quality capacitive multi touch queuing ticket dispenser with a modular look and feel combined with a multimedia support bringing customer flow and experience a big delight.
