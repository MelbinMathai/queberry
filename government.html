Government & Public Sector

From its conception, Queberry is designed Appointment Scheduling Software for Government Offices to work in a secure public environment while providing superior service quality to the residents as it offers and maintains a relaxed environment for visitors and employees.

Governments across the world are now moving to a mix of online services and walk-in services.

In a new technology world and a backdrop of the rise of the digitally oriented, on-demand, smart device-savvy citizen who wants to access the services they need through their preferred channels, Queberry helps manage this complex task by allowing integration and mobile app platform for digital appointment booking and queue management.

Automate workflow with an Integrated Appointment Solution

Government offices are one of the busiest places. When there are many visitors, it will be a daunting task for the staff to manage everything. Long wait lines can be stressful for both staff and visitors.

That’s where Government appointment scheduling software from Queberry works! Long lineups can be replaced with virtual queues with queue management systems for Government.

With appointment scheduling software for Government, the process of scheduling appointments can be simplified, allowing employees to create and manage appointments quickly.

Inform citizens in real time about their estimated wait times (get out of line or request more time). It also makes it possible for citizens to schedule and manage appointments online.

The automation of customer flow and the elimination of unnecessary steps will improve employee satisfaction.

Eliminate wait lines, prioritise individuals who are waiting, transfer citizens across departments, and facilitate online transactions.

Government appointment scheduling software makes it easy to quickly schedule, reschedule and cancel appointments. This saves time and resources, allowing staff to focus on more important tasks.
