Years of Experience

Since its inception, Queberry is growing in a fast pace to be best in achieving and specializing Customer Experience Solutions.

Queberry, digital transformation services, manages the customer journey with a range of products and solutions such as Queue Management Solutions, Digital Signage, Customer Feedback, Appointment Scheduling App that covers different stages of the customer journey from pre-arrival to visiting a branch and leaving feedback for advanced business analytics.

Our Vision

It is our strong belief that life gets much better with more time thus by providing excellent and fast customer journey solution we bring more time to our customers. Providing the best customer experience through simple and elegant solutions for businesses as well as their customers.
