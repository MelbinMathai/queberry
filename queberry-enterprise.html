Queberry Enterprise

Queberry Enterprise is a complete integrated customer journey management system designed and conceptualized to improve your customer experience, staff resourcing & optimization, and business opportunities.

The big data analytics across the whole organization and branch of networks gives deep insight of customer journey management.

Our Customer
Journey Management Features

Unlimited Services

Our customer journey management system offers complete services such as real-time insights into customer behaviour, predictive analytics, personalised customer journeys, automated customer segmentation, etc. All these services combine to help businesses achieve a more profitable, efficient and customer-centric approach to customer flow management.

Centralized System

Our digital journey management solution is a centralised system that helps streamline customer data and processes. Our system provides a single point of access to customer data and insights across all branches and networks, enabling the organisation to gain a deeper understanding of customer behaviour.

Touchless Queuing

Customer life cycle management solutions create a revolutionary way to manage customer experience while minimising physical contact. With our touchless queuing technology, customers can easily join a queue with a simple tap on their phone, and even book their time slot in advance.

Business Analytics

Our customer journey management system provides powerful business analytics to help you track, analyse, and optimise customer journeys. You can also track customer journey performance in real time. Our customer management system helps you maximise customer engagement, increase customer satisfaction, and ultimately drive more business growth.

Distributed Model

Queberry’s customer journey management system is designed to provide a unified and efficient customer experience across multiple locations including branches, stores, and other customer touchpoints. Our system is a modular and scalable platform, allowing businesses to tailor the solution to their specific needs.

Social Media Integration

Our customer journey management system provides social media integration allowing businesses to collect data on customer interests and preferences, and gain insights into customer buying patterns and behaviours. This integration also enables businesses to create powerful campaigns, track their success and measure the ROI of their efforts.

Queberry Enterprise Customer journey management system adapts all business requirements from one branch to a multi branch environment and supports immense scalability covering third party integration and easy adaptability as all the modules are designed to operate independently and seamlessly working with each other

Queberry Enterprise comes with several different modules working in conjunction, capturing every inputs of customer journey at different touch points, which will be processed by a very powerful business analytical engine. Using this business analytics and data, you will better understand your customer, their need and improve their expectation and branch experience.
