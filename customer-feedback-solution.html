Customer
Feedback Solution

In the new era of customer engagement, customers are won and lost in real time, and they all have different Moments of Truth. Queberry Customer Feedback Software Solution Offering your customers the choice of how and when to share their feedback response gives businesses more opportunities to reach your customers.

FeedInn is a multi-channel customer feedback solution which allows customer to give immediate simple feedback or a multi-layer feedback on how they experience the service and whole customer journey

Feature of Customer
Feedback Solution

Multiple Channel

Great customer service will get customers talking about your company, and multi-channel solutions will allow customers to provide feedback through multi-channels and increase customer satisfaction.

Integration Ready

The feedback management system enables interface with external devices and integrates seamlessly with other technologies. It provides a 360-degree understanding of your customer.

On the service counter

Customer feedback tools transform service counter feedback solutions, increase customer loyalty and satisfaction, and deliver customer feedback surveys via SMS, email, or mobile tickets.

SMS survey

SMS Survey enables you to create customized personalization SMS with your customers and measure customer satisfaction, e-commerce, delivery, and patient feedback information.

Multiple Questioner Format

Multiple questionnaires are the most commonly used customer feedback tools for gathering information and generating customer responses using a predetermined list of choices.

White Labelled and Customised

White label and customized features offer a fully customizable system to add your company logo, brand colour palette, change font and icons to ensure you’re always on brand.
