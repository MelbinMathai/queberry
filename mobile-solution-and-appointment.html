Mobile
Solution &
Appointment

Mobility has changed the way customer experience works, and Mobile App is a trendy modular channel that allows customers to choose Appointment Booking And Scheduling services on the go in a convenient way.

This appointment scheduling app offers the ability to check the nearest branch by maps or name, routes, and perceived wait time and book a service for a date and time by enabling service to anyone and anytime, which brings the customer closer to your business. Queberry provides a Touchless experience with an Appointment Booking And Scheduling App that eliminates the pain of waiting and provides a seamless customer experience.

Feature of Mobile
Solution & Appointment

On the go appointment booking

The online appointment booking system provides a user-friendly booking experience to customers, and customers can choose the days and timings of specific services anywhere in the world.

Brings mobility- Near Me & Google Direction

The appointment Scheduling app allows customers to self-schedule appointments directly from Google Search and Google Maps results. It provides a new channel to receive bookings and increase sales.

Virtual ticket in the branch

Virtual tickets help monitor and manage customer flow in the branch and allow insights into the current branch situation with a real-time dashboard; This increases employee productivity and efficiency.

Schedule a meeting

Queberry advanced appointment scheduling solution improves the work environment, customer experience, queueing and waiting time by enabling visitors to schedule a meeting.

Virtual Visit

Flexible virtual meeting solutions allow customers to book appointments and select virtual meeting services, enriching the wait time experience and creating more satisfied and happy customers.

White Labelled and Customised

The white-label appointment booking software solution lets you add your logo, change colors and fonts, and your business can fully utilize the app within your current client experience.
