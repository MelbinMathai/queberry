Touchless Queuing

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, businesses has to adapt the precautions as per new Covid norms and thus social distancing is a new normal along with hand hygiene. Virtual Queue Management System offer Customers to sit in a socially distanced waiting area and be notified on the mobile app or SMS/WhatsApp on the progress of the queue and their status

TouchlessQ is a virtual queuing solutions as technological initiatives taken by Queberry for a safer queuing considering Covid-19.

Features of
Touchless Queuing

Online Booking

Our virtual queuing system allows customers to schedule their bookings online, helps to plan and eliminate unscheduled consumers, and offers a better customer experience.

Maintain social distancing

Virtual queue management systems manage queues and reduce the number of waiting customers, and customers can be in line before arrival and get notified about queuing updates.

Customer Feedback

After customers visit, an invitation is sent for an online survey to inform you about customers’ opinions to improve your services and increase efficiency.

Messaging Service

Virtual queuing solutions notify customers of booking confirmations, deliver contactless tickets and appointment reminders via messaging and improve customer flow.

Non-touch queuing solution

  • By QR Code
  • By SMS
  • By WhatsApp and Social Media
  • By Apps and Web Appointment

Business Analytics

A web-based robust business analytics software solution drives your business with operational excellence, collects and generates data to life, suggesting operational improvements, future resource planning, and strategic performance improvement.

It covers all modules of Queberry business intelligence solutions by capturing data at all touch points and user engagement, compiling and consolidating data to provide an accurate and in-depth analysis of business KPIs and customer experience.

Feature of
Business Analytics

Robust Analytics Tool

Robust business intelligence tools put information front and center at your fingertips, allowing you to manage and analyze complex data, make decisions, and improve operations.

Data Model for Integration

Business analytics software support integration with other analytics and data visualization tools so data can be easily shared and analyzed wherever the data resides.

Out of the box reports

With out-of-the-box reports, you can create and broadcast reports on the fly without IT assistance and see the best and worst-performing segments of your business.

Custom report builder

Business intelligence solution reports allow users to convert data into knowledge. It offers scalable, customized analytics, and you can build custom reports based on your organization’s needs.

Live Dashboard

Our Live business intelligence dashboard offers a relevant and efficient understanding of a real-time overview of your business, enabling executives to make smarter, faster, and better decisions.

Alerts and Notifications

Instead of constantly monitoring your dashboard, our real-time data notifications and alerts tell you when something good or bad happens, and the system saves your work time.
