Queberry
Cloud Solution

Queberry cloud-based queue management system brings queuing or customer journey management as a SaaS model on a subscription based model.

Regardless what your needs are, Queberry cloud-based queue management system can be adapted and configured to match per requirement in a seamless one click. You just need to select what functionality you need, and we will take care of the configuration, hosting, and maintenance of the solution.

Partnering with Queberry Cloud Solutions for your customer journey and queuing solution means you no longer have to deal with IT-related matters and cost such as security, stability, system uptime and software maintenance as everything will be included in the subscription fee as per your plan selection.

Being on cloud system, new features upgrade with latest version will be guaranteed for your business to run without any additional cost.

Features of
Queberry Cloud Solutions

SAAS - Managed

Our cloud-based queue management system is a SaaS (Software as a Service) model that takes the hassle away from managing and maintaining your customer journey and queuing process. With the cloud-based queue system, you can focus on running your business and let the software manage the rest.

Private Cloud Support

Queberry’s cloud-based queue management system offers private cloud support. With this, you get guaranteed security and privacy, and your data remains secure and protected. The private cloud solution also allows you to customise and configure your system as per your specific requirements and business needs.

Azure & AWS

Our cloud-based queue management system is powered by the reliable and secure cloud platforms of Microsoft Azure and Amazon Web Services (AWS). With our cloud-based queue system, you can process and manage customer queues efficiently and securely, ensuring a seamless customer experience.

Multi-branch Solution

Our cloud-based queueing system offers a multi-branch solution tailored to meet the needs of larger businesses. This solution allows businesses to manage queues across multiple locations from a single dashboard. It also offers the ability to manage customer queues remotely and provide a consistent customer experience across all business branches.

Scalability and Easy Integration

Queberry’s cloud-based queue management system is highly scalable and easy to integrate with existing systems. It allows you to quickly scale up or down your customer journey management solution as the demand for your services changes.

Free Release Upgrades

Our cloud-based queue management system offers free release upgrades to all our customers. These upgrades are released regularly and can include anything from new features and bug fixes to improved performance. We strive to ensure that our customers are always running the most recent version of our system.

Queberry Cloud Solution offers the same top notch features, functionality and flexibility as Queberry Enterprise and can also be made tailored or customized as per your need and requirement.

It gives you more time on what you do best i.e. serving your customer with delight without getting into the management of your queuing solution adding more value to your business. The cloud solution supports high availability and scalability to next level.
